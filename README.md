# BattleshipPythonProject

Hello, Welcome to our Battleship Project!

How-To-Play:

In the game, you and the enemy have one length 4 warship, two length 3, two length 2, and three length 1. You choose where to put them on the grid map. Then, in each turn, you can select which grid square to attack. If you get a hit on a square, the grid square will turn into a red X. Otherwise, it will turn into a white circle. Beware, the computer knows when it has hit your ship, and will continue to hit in the same area until it destroys your ship. You must destroy all of the enemy ships to win. If the enemy gets all of your ships before then, you lose and go back to the menu, where your wins/losses will be displayed.

Enjoy playing!
